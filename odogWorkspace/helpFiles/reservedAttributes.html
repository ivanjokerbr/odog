Reserved attributes

The following attributes have special meanings and are reserved for the system. The names are case sensitive.


Toplevel : when associated with a composite component, indicates that it is the level-zero component of the project. There can be more than one such component, and they are the starting point for code generation.

ISEM : associates a composite component with an interaction semantics.

delayed: depending on the associated ISem that governs the component, this attribute has different meanings:

    DE: associated to an output port, indicates that all events produced by this port will allways be into the future.

    DF: associated to an output port, indicates that data is procuded at the initialization step.

nonstrict: under the SR ISem, indicates that the associated component is Non-Strict.
